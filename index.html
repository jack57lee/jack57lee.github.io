General

Jian Li is a Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. He obtained a B.Eng. degree with the Outstanding Honor from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in June, 2015.

His research interests include deep learning applied to natural language processing as well as programming languages. From July 2019 to March 2020, he was visiting at Stanford University advised by Prof. Monica Lam and working on the Almond Conversational AI.
